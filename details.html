Rome

Rome today is one of the most important tourist destinations of the world, due to the incalculable immensity of its archaeological and artistic treasures. Among the most significant resources are the many museums - Musei Capitolini, the Vatican Museums and the Galleria Borghese and others dedicated to modern and contemporary art - aqueducts, fountains, churches, palaces, historical buildings, the monuments and ruins of the Roman Forum, and the Catacombs.

Main spots -

  • The Pantheon, The Colosseum
  • The Victor Emmanuel II Monument
  • Ostia Lido beach
